Biography

Mosunmola is the second child of the Shode family. She pursued a degree in Mass Communications at Olabisi Onabanjo University and has successfully completed her studies. Like Lukman, Mosunmola attended Bola Nursery and Primary School for her primary education and Mayflower Secondary School for her secondary education. Mosunmola is a passionate fashion designer, a field where she has demonstrated exceptional creativity and skill.
