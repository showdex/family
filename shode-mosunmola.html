Biography

Mosunmola is the second child of the Shode family. She completed her degree in Public Administration at Olabisi Onabanjo University. Alongside her academic achievements, Mosunmola is a talented fashion designer. Her passion for fashion is complemented by her interests in sewing and traveling.

Education

Primary School: Bola Nursery and Primary School (2006 - 2012)

Secondary School: Mayflower Secondary School (2012 - 2018)

Tertiary Education: Olabisi Onabanjo University (2018 - 2022)

Department: Public Administration

Programme: B.Sc. Public Administration

Hobbies & Interests

Mosunmola’s hobbies include sewing, where she creates and designs unique clothing pieces, and traveling, which allows her to explore new cultures and gain inspiration for her fashion work.

Achievements

  • Graduated with a degree in Public Administration
  • Established her own fashion line
  • Featured in local fashion shows
