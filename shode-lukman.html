Biography

Lukman is the third child of the Shode family. He has a strong passion for science and technology, always curious and eager to learn. After completing secondary school, Lukman stayed home for a few years before enrolling at Olusegun Agagu University of Science & Technology, Okitipupa, where he is now in his 300 level, studying Electrical and Electronics Engineering.
